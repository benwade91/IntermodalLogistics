About Us

Intermodal Logistics Inc. is a full-service logistics company based in Oakland, California. We offer a wide range of services to meet the needs of our clients, including warehousing, transportation, and customs clearance. Our team of experts is here to help you with all of your logistics needs.

We are committed to providing our clients with the highest level of service and support. Our facilities are equipped with the latest technology to ensure the safety and security of your goods. Whether you are moving goods across town or around the world, we have the expertise and resources to get the job done right.
